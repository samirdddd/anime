One Piece

Plot Summary

Monkey D. Luffy sets out to become the King of the Pirates and find the legendary treasure known as One Piece. Along the way, he and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, encounter dangerous enemies, powerful allies, and uncover secrets about the world.
