Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series (no 1) written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It follows the story of high school student Yuji Itadori, who becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer after eating a cursed object containing a powerful curse spirit. Alongside his fellow sorcerers, including Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, Yuji fights against cursed spirits and other supernatural threats to protect humanity.
