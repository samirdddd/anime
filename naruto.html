Naruto

Plot summary

Naruto is a popular Japanese anime series that follows the story of a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja in his village. Along with his teammates Sasuke and Sakura, Naruto goes on missions and trains to become stronger while facing various challenges and enemies.
