Attack on Titan

In a world where humanity lives inside giant walls to protect themselves from man-eating Titans, a young boy named Eren Yeager vows to eliminate the Titans and avenge his mother's death. Along with his childhood friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert, Eren joins the military to fight against the Titans using special gear that allows them to move around in the air. They soon discover that there is more to the Titans than they originally thought, and they must uncover the secrets behind them to save humanity. Will they be able to do so?
